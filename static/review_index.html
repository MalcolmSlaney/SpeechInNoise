Scoring Instructions

This task will consist of scoring clinical tests of speech recognition. Please use headphones. Score these as you would in routine clinical practice. You may only listen to each playback once.

The key words are displayed to the right of the audio. Score each word correct (blue) or incorrect (red) by clicking it. The "All" button marks all key words correct and "None" marks all key words incorrect.

Submit and progress by clicking the "Next Audio" button.
